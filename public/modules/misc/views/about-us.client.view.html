Mission Statement

Focali Optics was founded by a team of optical and ecommerce industry veterans dedicated to providing a high-quality and fashionable medical device with the best technology to facilitate and promote eye health. Far too often, the decision of which product to purchase is based solely on the frame and fashion choices. While fashion is certainly important with something that’s worn on the face, attention should also be paid to ensuring that the lenses and coatings provided maximize visual acuity and promote eye health. That’s why all of our optical lenses are offered with Trivex® lenses, which have a similar impact-resistant profile but a much higher Abbe value (a measure of optical clarity) than do the polycarbonate lenses offered by many online competitors. In addition, all of our sunglasses include polarized lenses with backside anti-reflective coating, because we feel strongly that this is the best option available for eye health and comfort. The team at Focali also believes in the importance of giving back to the community, which is why we partner with charitable organizations to promote eye health worldwide.
